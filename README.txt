== Description ==

👉 [Official GitHub Repository](https://github.com/wptinysolution/wc-quantity-field) 👈

This plugin adds a user-friendly quantity field to the shop, product, cart, and checkout pages of WooCommerce stores, allowing customers to easily adjust product quantities and manage their cart for a seamless shopping experience.

== 🏆 Quantity Field on the Shop Page ==

Easily display a quantity field on the shop page of your WooCommerce store, allowing customers to adjust the quantity of products before adding them to the cart.

== 🏆 Quantity Field on the Checkout Page ==

Enable customers to update product quantities using WooCommerce’s standard quantity field directly on the checkout page. Additionally, customers can delete products for a smoother checkout experience.

== 🏆 Quantity Field on the Cart Page ==

Offer a clean and user-friendly layout for the quantity field on the cart page, making it simple for customers to update quantities or remove items from their cart.

== 🏆 Quantity Field on the Product Page ==

Provide an intuitive and well-designed quantity field on individual product pages, allowing customers to easily select their desired quantity before adding items to the cart.
